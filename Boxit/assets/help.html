1. Introduction

What is BOXit?

Android Boxit is a small logic game for playing occasionally. It is one of the games that easy and quick to learn but sometimes hard to handle. If you have fun in puzzling, Android Boxit may be for you.

2. System requirements

Android Boxit 1.0 has been developed and tested on Android version 2.1 and above. The requirements are as:

  • a system with Android 2.1 or above.
  • Touch based input is required.

4. The game

The objective of the puzzle is to clear the tiles as much as possible by moving them around. The tokens can be dragged onto the same-colored or equivalent tokens one over another when the destination token is in the same line or column of the dragged token.

An ideal objective should be to reach one token. To achieve that lot of practice and maneuvering skills are required ;-)

Undo button allows to go to the previous state until there are no more old moves.
